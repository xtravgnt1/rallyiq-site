Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  ·  Season 2026

Our team uses RallyIQ — an AI-powered analytics tool — to help athletes improve through data-driven coaching. We value the privacy of our players and families above all else.

What Is RallyIQ?

RallyIQ is a virtual assistant coach. It analyzes match footage to track volleyball-specific patterns and generate coaching insights. Instead of spending hours manually reviewing footage, the AI provides tactical suggestions instantly so coaches can focus on player development.

Our Privacy Promises

01

No Facial Recognition

RallyIQ does not use facial recognition or biometric tracking of any kind. Players are identified by jersey number and visible name text only.

02

Your Data Is Never Sold

RallyIQ never sells, rents, or shares player data or video footage with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.

03

We Don't Train AI on Your Data

Your footage and player data are never used to train AI models. Analysis is performed and results are returned to the coach — that is the only purpose.

04

Private Coaching Use Only

Footage is used strictly for internal coaching and player development. It is never made public.

05

Automatic Deletion

Video footage is automatically deleted from our servers within 90 days of upload.

06

Your Right to Delete

Any parent or coach can request complete deletion of their data at any time by contacting legal@rallyiq.app.

How the AI Works

The AI analyzes footage the way a coach does — tracking player positioning, movements, and patterns to generate tactical suggestions. It identifies players by jersey numbers only, not by face or personal information. Analysis is performed using secure AI services from Google, Anthropic, and OpenAI, who are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

Who Processes the Data?

The following services are used to operate RallyIQ:

  • Supabase: stores account and roster information
  • Google Cloud: processes video footage for AI analysis
  • DigitalOcean: secure server infrastructure
  • RevenueCat: manages coaching subscriptions
  • AI Providers (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI): generate coaching insights

All providers are bound by data processing agreements and cannot use your data beyond providing this service.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Our database uses Row-Level Security, meaning each coach can only access their own team's data.

COPPA Compliance

RallyIQ is designed for adult coaches. Players do not create accounts and do not interact with the App directly. Coaches are responsible for obtaining appropriate parental consent before recording minors.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how RallyIQ handles your data, please contact us at legal@rallyiq.app.
